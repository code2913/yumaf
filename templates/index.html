Know About Us

We are pleased to offer our services in freight forwarding & custom clearance to handle your import or export shipments

The company was established and incorporated on 4th July, 2011 with a registration number 84301.
We strive to be one of Tanzania’s fastest growing freight forwarding and clearing companies. Our company is a member of TAFFA (Tanzania Freight Forwarders Association) with employees qualified on customs and freight forwarding.

YUMAF is an authorized service provider in the field of Customs, Clearing, Freight Forwarding, We also advise on Transportation and Logistics. We provide office support to shipping lines, non-vessel operating carriers, and manufacturer and Merchant exporters .

We clear incoming and outgoing goods by Sea, Air, Road and Railway. All our staff members are trained and equipped to understand each Customers’ need. We relay real time information on goods to our clients during all stages of the clearing process.
